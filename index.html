Hi! I’m a technical artist pursuing a graduate degree in Entertainment Technology at Carnegie Mellon University’s Entertainment Technology Center (ETC).

I did my undergrad in computer science (with a focus in HCI) and have worked as a traditional animator on Pakistan’s first hand-drawn animated feature film, 'The Glassworker'.

My experience with animation has helped me fortify my understanding of art fundamentals, human anatomy, character design and character animation. This, combined with my formal CS and HCI education, puts me in a unique position to easily communicate ideas across inter-disciplinary teams, empathize and communicate with artists, identify barriers in art pipelines and build tools and rigs to alleviate these barriers.
