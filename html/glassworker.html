Muhammad Bin Tahir Mir

The Glassworker

Mano Animation Studios is working exclusively on Pakistan’s first hand-drawn animated feature film, “The Glassworker”.

When: July 2017 - March 2018
Where: Mano Animation Studios, Karachi



The Story:
Mano aims to bring hand-drawn animation to Pakistan—to make it Pakistan’s own. The studio believes in the product it’s working on and has gone to great lengths to preserve it’s artistic integrity. It’s showing young Pakistanis that animation is something they can aspire to get into; animation is something they can make a career out of.

I joined in July of 2017. Here, I've learnt the principles of animation and have gotten hands-on experience working as an animator on numerous shots for "The Glassworker". My contributions to the shots have included key-framing, rough animation, line-work, colouring and shading. All my film-related work, however, is protected under a non-disclosure agreement and cannot be shared unless made public by the studio itself.
